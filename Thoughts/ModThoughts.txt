Character Mechanics
    Embed.  Card is removed from play, and associated with a debuff added to the enemy.  Returned to hand on death of enemy (and maybe other means).
    Most scaling through debuffs.  Low power count.
    Lots of draw.  Lots of discard.
    Sigils.  Unplayable.  Has on discard effects.

    No Ethereal, Exhaust, or card generation
    No retain, innate, or deck manip
    No Healing
    No Damage AoE
    No major poison/burning adjacent mechanics
    No temp str, dex.  Use vigor, vitality.

New Debuffs
    Hobble
	Applies 33% less weak, vuln, frail, strength, block, and healing
    Drowned
	Enemy loses x extra hp per loss (everything, not just attacks).  Lose 25% stacks per turn

New Buffs
    Streamlined
	When you draw a card or gain energy, draw/gain 1 more and reduce this by 1.

Starting Relic
    Rune of ice
	Click to draw a card and discard a card.  One charge.  Gain a charge when you shuffle.
    Rune of living water
	Click to draw a card and discard a card.  Start with 2 extra charges.

Remove Relics
    Strange Spoon, Dead Branch, anything else exhaust based
    ChemicalX

Potions

	Apply 3 hobble
    Water Bucket, Uncommon
	Apply 4 drowned
    Shark Fin Potion, Rare
	Gain 6 streamlined
